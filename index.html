⚡ Breaking News Challenge — Group Activity
Report It
Your Way.
A role-play activity exploring how different communication systems share the same story — and why it matters.
📡 Today's Breaking Story
"A Meteor Just Landed in
the School Parking Lot!"
Your Groups
📻 Radio
Write an audio-only script. No visuals, no images — only words people can hear. Keep it under 60 seconds.
📺 Television
Write a TV news script that describes both what you say AND what viewers will see on screen.
📰 Print
Write a short newspaper article with a headline, who/what/where/when/why, and as much detail as possible.
🌐 Internet
Write a social media post. Include a caption, hashtags, and 3 "replies" from the public.
How It Works
1
Get into your groupYour teacher will assign each group one communication system.
2
Report the storyUse ONLY the rules for your system. Radio can't show pictures. Print can't be instant. Stick to it!
3
Present to the classEach group shares their report. Act it out if you can!
4
Discuss togetherWhat was easy? What was frustrating? What couldn't you do?
💬 Discussion Questions — Be Ready to Answer These
Which system reached the most people the fastest?
Which system gave the most detail? Why does that matter?
Which was the only system where the audience could respond?
Standard: 7.MS-ETS3-2(MA) — Compare benefits and drawbacks of communication systems
⏱ ~30 Minutes
